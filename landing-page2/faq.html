Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Public AI for Libraries?

Public AI for Libraries is a platform that provides educational institutions with access to a comprehensive suite of AI-powered tools and services. We aim to democratize AI access for libraries, schools, and educational organizations.

Who can use these services?

Our services are designed for libraries, educational institutions, students, researchers, and educators. We focus on providing equitable access to AI tools for educational purposes.

Is there a cost to use these services?

Many of our services are available at no cost or with educational discounts. We believe in making AI tools accessible to educational institutions regardless of their budget constraints.

Technical Questions

What AI tools are available?

We provide access to various AI services including coding environments (Replit), conversational AI (Public AI Chat), image generation (Midjourney), music creation (Suno), video editing (RunwayML), and presentation tools (Gamma).

Do I need technical expertise to use these tools?

No! Our tools are designed to be user-friendly and accessible to users of all technical levels. We provide tutorials, documentation, and support to help you get started.

How do I access the services?

Simply click on any of the service cards on our main page to access the respective AI tools. Each service opens in a new tab and provides direct access to the platform.

Privacy and Security

Is my data safe?

Yes, we prioritize user privacy and data security. Each service we link to has its own privacy policy, and we work with partners who maintain high standards for data protection.

Do you store my conversations or content?

We do not store your conversations or content. Each AI service handles data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing each service's privacy policy before use.

Support and Training

Do you provide training for library staff?

Yes! We offer resources and guidance to help library staff integrate AI tools into their services. Contact us to learn about training opportunities and resources.

How can I get help if I'm having issues?

You can reach out to us through our contact channels, or use the AI chat feature on our platform for immediate assistance with common questions.

Can I suggest new features or services?

Absolutely! We welcome feedback and suggestions from our library community. Your input helps us improve and expand our services.

Implementation

How do I implement this in my library?

Implementation is straightforward - simply bookmark our platform and share it with your patrons. We provide guidance on integrating AI tools into library services and educational programs.

Do you offer institutional accounts?

We're working on institutional account features. Contact us to discuss options for your specific institution's needs.
